## Full Description (4000 char max)

FlockFree Navigation is a privacy-first navigation app that helps you make informed routing decisions with situational awareness of ALPR (Automated License Plate Reader) cameras on your route.

**Camera Awareness**
• 100,000+ known ALPR camera locations from public OpenStreetMap data
• Camera orientation cones showing which direction each camera faces
• Real-time proximity alerts when approaching known cameras
• Camera proximity widget showing count and distance to nearest cameras
• Optional WiFi Flock detection for Flock Safety camera beacons

**Route Avoidance**
• Iterative camera avoidance routing — finds routes that minimize camera exposure
• Partial avoidance reporting when full avoidance isn't possible
• Avoidance tradeoff summary ("Avoids 5 cameras · +2 min")

**Modern Navigation**
• Google Maps-inspired map styling with day/night themes
• Turn-by-turn navigation with lane guidance
• Automatic 3D tilt during navigation
• Search-along-route for gas, food, coffee, parking, and EV charging
• Destination arrival preview with side-of-street indication
• Transparent buildings that hide before turns

**Android Auto**
• Full Android Auto navigation support
• Turn-by-turn directions on your car's head unit

**Optional Companion Hardware**
• CYD-Flock-You companion device support for RF-based camera detection
• BLE connection to ESP32-based hardware for field detections
• Detection review workflow before submitting to OpenStreetMap

**Privacy First**
• No personal data collected
• No analytics, no ads, no tracking
• Location stays on your device
• Camera database is bundled (not collected from users)
• Optional features (TomTom traffic, OSM reporting) use your own API keys

**Open Source**
FlockFree Navigation is built on OsmAnd and is fully open source.
Source code: https://github.com/yetisoldier/FlockFree-Navigation

**Permissions Explained**
• Location: Navigation and camera proximity alerts
• Bluetooth: Optional CYD companion hardware connection
• Wi-Fi: Optional Flock Safety camera beacon detection
• Foreground services: Navigation, downloads, and BLE companion
